Privacy Policy for Sobrio

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Sobrio ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Sobrio mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve our service:

  • Name you provide during onboarding
  • Sobriety start date and tracking data
  • Check-in logs, mood, and craving entries
  • Drinking habit information provided during setup
  • Camera and video access (for Future Self Video feature)
  • Device information for notification delivery

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize the app experience
  • To send you notifications and reminders you have requested
  • To calculate your sobriety streak and progress statistics
  • To detect behavioral patterns to support your recovery
  • To process subscription payments via Apple App Store and Google Play

Data Storage

Your personal data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your personal sobriety data to external servers.

Payments

Sobrio offers subscriptions processed through Apple App Store In-App Purchases and Google Play In-App Purchases. We do not store your payment information. All payments are handled by Apple and Google according to their respective privacy policies.

Third Party Services

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymous usage data. Please refer to RevenueCat's privacy policy at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy for more information.

GDPR — Your Rights

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to object to processing of your data

Children's Privacy

Sobrio is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
